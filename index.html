About the Game

Migong is an engaging maze puzzle game that challenges players to navigate through increasingly complex labyrinths. Test your problem-solving skills, spatial awareness, and perseverance as you find the path from start to finish.

How to Play

Navigate through the maze by using the arrow keys on desktop or swiping on mobile devices. Choose your difficulty level and maze size to customize your gaming experience. The goal is simple yet challenging: find your way from the start point to the exit while avoiding dead ends and discovering the optimal path.

Multiple Difficulty Levels

From beginner to expert, choose the challenge that matches your skill level.

Customizable Maze Size

Select different maze dimensions for varied gameplay experiences.

Cross-Platform Compatibility

Play seamlessly on desktop computers, tablets, and mobile phones.
